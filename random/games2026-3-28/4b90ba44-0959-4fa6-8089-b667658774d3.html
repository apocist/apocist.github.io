Description

Game Overview

Step into the role of a commander in a vast fantasy world.

Manage, train, and lead your heroes to forge the strongest Alliance squad.

Key Features

1. Innovative Multi-Character Management System

Unlike traditional RPGs that focus on a single hero, Panoptica allows you to nurture up to eight heroes simultaneously.

Each hero explores a different region independently, embarking on their own unique adventure.

2. Real-Time Four-View Battle Observation Mode

Through the four-panel Command Mode, you can monitor multiple ongoing battles in real time.

Step in instantly during emergencies to adjust your strategy, or focus on upgrading your heroes' equipment and skills when the situation is stable.

3. Party Dungeons

While individual heroes hunt autonomously across the field, you can form two-hero parties to challenge high-difficulty dungeons.

Experiment with combinations such as tank and DPS, or mage and healer, to defeat powerful bosses that would be impossible to face alone—earning both exciting rewards and the satisfaction of cooperative play.

4. The Essence of Strategic RPG Management

Go beyond simple leveling and become a true commander of heroes.

Assign characters to optimal battlefields based on their classes and traits, distribute equipment efficiently, and manage skill trees to maximize synergy.

With precise auto-battle customization, you can design and control the battlefield exactly as you envision it.

The satisfaction of growing up to eight heroes simultaneously delivers a new sense of accomplishment never before felt in traditional RPGs.

5. Aesthetic of Medieval Fantasy and TRPG Charm

Featuring parchment-style visuals reminiscent of classic tabletop RPGs, Panoptica digitally recreates the nostalgic feeling of rolling dice with friends around a table.

Each region offers its own monsters and treasures, and as your exploration deepens, a richer world and lore unfold before you.
