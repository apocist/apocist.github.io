Description

Life is Feudal: MMO is a subscription-based MMO set in a medieval world of thrilling risks and unyielding realism. From intense PvP combat and large-scale guild wars to politics and diplomacy, the game is alive with possibilities. With unique crafting mechanics and professions, players can master their domain, build formidable fortresses, and dive deep into guild life for an immersive experience. The return of this legendary game opens the vast lands of Abella once more, offering players the chance to ascend through skillful swordplay or masterful wealth accumulation.


With limitless terraforming and free construction systems, transform any territory into a haven, or construct sky-high fortresses, prosperous villages, and grand fortresses in any configuration. Your creativity is your only limit.




Life is Feudal: MMO offers unparalleled sandbox freedom on an MMO scale. Engage in intense political maneuvering, thrilling battles, and immerse yourself in the complexity of medieval professions. Discover the depth of the crafting system, and shape your own destiny in this expansive world.




Life is Feudal: MMO takes you beyond battle and politics, offering immersive experiences like planting, harvesting crops, hunting or breeding a variety of different animals. Survival has never been this real.




The combat in Life is Feudal: MMO is visceral and real. Weapons must physically hit to cause damage and physics parameters are considered. Stand with your allies in prearranged battles, or test your mettle in solo survival arenas. Victory rewards the spoils of war: claim everything your fallen enemy possessed.




Immerse yourself in true political sandbox gameplay. Engage in authentic, large-scale sieges, guild wars, alliances, and real map land control tactics. Victory means the power to conquer, tax other players' guilds, and even rule whole kingdoms.




In Life is Feudal: MMO, you're never alone. Join one of the hundreds of guilds and shape the beautiful 36x36 km map alongside other players. Every corner of the world is yours to discover.




Life is Feudal: MMO offers complete character customization, from appearance to equipment. Choose your medieval profession and develop it in a way that suits your playstyle. Discover and unlock hundreds of blueprints to enhance your crafting skills, creating a unique experience tailored just for you.




In Life is Feudal: MMO, player feedback is paramount. We value the community's insights, regularly host Q&A sessions, and are always open to suggestions and critique. Players have the opportunity to shape the game's development, directly influencing the evolution of the game world.
