Description

A Toge Greenhouse Project

Project KILLDANCE was created during Toge Greenhouse 2023, an internal game jam held by Toge Productions to create new, innovative, and experimental games.

Become the spectral death

A Noir tactical action game featuring a one-army strategy and instant-death combat. Slowly observe your surroundings, choreograph each killing move, finish your mission, and leave no survivors.

Features

Plan your movement and perform your attack cards to clear each stage.

Time is frozen in between your actions, use this opportunity to dodge bullets, take cover, and kill enemies effectively.

Draft new cards to learn new skills and adapt to your missions.

Once you complete your mission, replay your performance and watch your KILLDANCE.
