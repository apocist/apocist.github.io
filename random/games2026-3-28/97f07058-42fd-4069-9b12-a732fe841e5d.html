Description

Guardians Frontline is a sci-fi VR shooter that makes you a soldier and a commander. Fight off enemies and build bases in a single-player and co-op campaign, plus PvP game modes with full cross-play support.

A UNIQUE SCI-FI SHOOTER

Guardians Frontline blends action-packed gameplay with real-time strategy elements. Explore a massive arsenal of weapons but also build structures & units. Lead the charge on the battlefield as you simultaneously drop huge mechs straight into combat!


A RANGE OF WEAPONS & VEHICLES

As a Guardian of the federation, you have access to a huge range of weaponry and tools. Shoot the Gravity Gun to smash your enemies against the cliffs, and maneuver both mechs and spaceships to wreak havoc from both the ground and sky!


GUARDIAN FRIENDS… AND FOES

Play solo or in co-op as you defend the federation. Guardians Frontline’s campaign can be played with up to 4 players in co-operative mode, while up to 8 players can battle it out in tense and strategic PvP fights. Full cross-play support is included throughout.


A FLOOD OF USER GENERATED CONTENT

A comprehensive, easy-to-use in-game level editor allows anyone to build their own maps and make them public for the community to enjoy, leading to almost infinite replay value.
