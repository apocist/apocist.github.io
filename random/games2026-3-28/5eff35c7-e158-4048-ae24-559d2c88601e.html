Description

Violent Horror Stories 2 delivers a fresh lineup of horror games from different developers. Each title puts its own spin on the theme of confinement, complete with distinctive visuals, gameplay, and story. Expect roughly 30 minutes of playtime per game.

Last year the pursuit‑themed anthology featured No, I'm not a Human, I ate old man's liver now this b***ch is mine, Sensation, All along the toon tower.

This time we are packing in even more games from an even wider range of authors, all centered on the new theme.

GAMES FROM THE ANTHOLOGY:

  • You have [x] missed calls

    After getting ahold of a classified documents folder, you isolate yourself in an old friend's apartment. As missed calls begin to pile up, decide which caller to meet at the day's end — but be wary! Some are not who they seem.

  • Hi, What's Your Emergency

    Based on real events. Story about how hard it is for a child trapped in an abusive home to get help. Barricade the door and fight through the hotline phone tree, where a wrong choice costs precious seconds.

  • Coin Locker Baby

    As a small forgotten child explore the life that was denied to you.

  • HeartWood

    The Great Evil has been defeated, but its final curse has looped the forest and driven your friends insane. In the guise of the Dream Girl, you must gather the scattered fragments of your former team.

  • Strike from the Skies

    After only a few hours, an unknown enemy has captured almost everything. Our last hope lies with artillery.  Use the telegraph to transmit coordinates and prevent the enemy from reaching the civilians.

  • Padishah's lady

    In the harem, all the girls are fighting for the attention of the Padishah. And you are one of these concubines. Charm everyone to survive and protect your future child.

  • The Village and the Dead

    A mysterious death took place in a remote village. Everyone has their own motives and their own dark past. To solve the case, you'll have to dig deeper... Even if the truth breaks you.

  • Coupon for a Million

    Welcome to the TV show: navigate the maze, complete tasks with the brand new phone, find the coveted coupon – but the organizers won't give you an easy win.

  • Down the Stairs

    Daily life of a person with dementia.

  • The Exit is Inside

    To escape from captivity in the morgue, a surgeon will have to learn to speak with the dead in the language of scalpel and tweezers.

  • Deadman’s Hand: Hold'em

    A dark, immersive visual novel blending horror elements with a Texas Hold'em poker simulation. Every player at the table is hiding something and it's not just their cards. Your life is on the line. Take all the chips or face the gunfire. This isn’t a casino. It’s a deathtrap.
