Description

You are the Exterminator on a crusade against the spider horde taking over the multiverse.

Play solo or with friends via online co-op for up to 4 players, blast through the story campaign, and show these pests who’s boss. Or, jump into a frenetic Humans vs Spiders PvP mode for some laughs and screams.

Whatever you choose, one thing is certain – it’s time to KILL IT WITH FIRE!!!

Wage war on the eight-legged monsters with an arsenal of 45 weapons and gadgets worthy of the Exterminator. Will your team smash the pests with a newspaper, burn them to a crisp, rev up the minigun, unleash a rocket launcher, or wield a laser sword?

Embark on an epic spider hunt across seven distinct worlds. Exterminate the pesky arachnids in a mysterious manor, liberate a town in the Wild West, explore cyberspace, and descend into Spider Hell. Every level is a unique battle against the spiders!

Brew magic potions in a bubbling cauldron. Demolish a megapolis as a kaiju-sized madman. Defend a cacti-owned saloon from waves of spiders. Win a drone race in virtual reality. There are more than 60 challenges to complete!

‘Okay, all this spider-hunting sounds fun, but what if I want to play as a spider?’ you might ask. And yes, you can indeed play as a spider in an online 8-player PvP mode called Spider Hunt. It’s actual humans vs actual arachnids (also controlled by humans)!

Key Features

  • 7 dimensions to save from the spider onslaught

  • 60+ unique in-game challenges to complete

  • 45 weapons and gadgets to unlock and craft

  • A campaign that’s fully playable solo or in 4-player co-op

  • Slapstick plot written by Miles Luna (RWBY, Red vs Blue)

  • 'Spider Hunt’ PvP multiplayer mode for up to 8 players

  • 20+ hours of content with at least 3 loud laughs guaranteed

  • 15.7 billion spiders to exterminate across the multiverse
