Description

Bloobs Adventure Idle is a massive RuneScape-inspired idle RPG built for serious long-term progression.

Inspired by RuneScape, Bloobs Adventure Idle is a full idle RPG built around training skills, fighting bosses, and letting your character progress while you do other things.

Skilling and Crafting

Train 24 skills — from Woodcutting, Mining, and Cooking to Magic, Thieving, and BeastMastery. Every skill feeds into the others, creating a layered web of progression where every action strengthens your character.

Push further with the prestige system, resetting skills for permanent bonuses and scaling your account into true endgame power.

Combat and Equipment

Combat is automated — but power is earned.

Prepare your build, craft stronger equipment, and challenge bosses designed to test your stats and gear. Each victory unlocks tougher encounters, better rewards, and rare unique drops that can redefine your progression.

Progression is constant. Power is scalable. The climb never stops.

Codex, Beastiary, Thief’s Log, Item Compendium, Achievements, and Souldex

Bloobs rewards players who love depth.

Track every enemy in the Beastiary, discover rare loot in the Thief’s Log, and catalog your progress in the Item Compendium. Dive into the Souldex — a collection of over 500 souls to unlock, each with unique variations and bonuses.

Whether you chase completion or focus purely on power progression, there’s always another milestone waiting.

Banked Time System

Idle on your schedule.

Bloobs stores up to 96 hours of offline time, giving you a powerful reserve of saved progress. Spend that banked time to accelerate the entire game — pushing skills, combat, and crafting forward at high speed whenever you choose.

You don’t just idle. You decide when the pace increases.

Bloobs Adventure Idle combines familiar RuneScape-inspired skilling with layered systems designed for long-term progression. Prestige, skill synergy, and exploration come together in a steady loop that continues to grow with you.
