Description

The esports classic Tower Tag comes home.

TOWER TAG isn't just another VR shooter—it’s a dynamic cyber-sport experience. From over 200 VR arcades worldwide, we bring the intuitive movement and tactical depth directly to your home.

Adrenaline Without the Nausea

Say goodbye to motion sickness. Our intuitive grappling hook mechanic makes rapid movement feel completely natural. Swing from tower to tower, maintain your orientation, and focus entirely on the fight. Speed is your ally, and surprise is your weapon.

Your Body is the Controller

We kept the controls intentionally simple to maximize immersion. In TOWER TAG, you don’t just control a character—you are the character.

  • Physical Cover: Use your real-world space! Duck behind towers, lean out for the perfect shot, and dodge enemy lasers with your own body.

  • Safe Space: The game is designed to allow safe and free movement, even in smaller play spaces ("Small Room Scale").

Strategic & Impactful

Team up, capture towers, and control the arena. It’s not just about reflexes; it’s about tactical precision.

  • Map Control: Capturing towers grants you cover and opens up new movement routes.

  • Teamplay: Coordinate with your squad to flank enemies or lure them into traps.

Features & Content

  •  Seamless Multiplayer: Enjoy Quick Match and Custom Matches. Always find a match!

  •  Modes for Every Style: Choose from three intense game modes:

    • Deathmatch: The classic—Free-for-all or Team vs. Team.

    • Elimination: Survival of the fittest.

    • Goal Tower: A tactical mode focused on area control and objective capture.

  • Match Variety: Whether it's intense 1v1 duels, 2v2, 3v3, or chaotic 4v4 battles—anything goes.

  • Intelligent Bots: No players online or just want to train? Practice against adaptive AI opponents that will genuinely challenge you.

  • Ranked Play: Show the world your skills and climb the global leaderboards.

  • Extended Immersion: Full support for bHaptics vests to let you feel every hit.
