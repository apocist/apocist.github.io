Description

Grab your hammers and pound the drums to epic tracks, from celtic rock to viking power metal. With dozens of official songs and thousands of community-made tracks, enjoy hours of fun as you play in perfect sync to carve your name on top of the leaderboard and race against your rivals.

⚡KEY FEATURES:

  • 46 songs in the base game from bands like Alestorm, Gloryhammer, Electric Callboy

  • 7 DLC adding 49 extra tracks from bands like Sabaton, Miracle of Sound, Nightwish, The Offspring

  • Easy to play, hard to master with 3 difficulty levels for each song

  • 4500+ community-made tracks via custom song support

  • Public or private online PvP multiplayer (up to 6 players)

  • Local split-screen mode for couch competition (up to 4 players)

  • Solo races against your own best records as ghost ships

  • VR compatible

  • Cross-platform multiplayer (PC & VR players together)

  • Lots of stylised Nordic environments inspired by Viking mythology
