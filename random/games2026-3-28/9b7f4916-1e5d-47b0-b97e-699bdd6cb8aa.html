Description

Defeat the Dragon Master -- and the rest

When a powerful, ruthless master emerges in the world of martial arts, it awakens many emotions. From the ambitious master looking for a challenge, the jealous emperor who wants to be the sole ruler, or even to the wronged peasant sworn to revenge -- it brings out many people to fight each other and achieve their own goals.

Step into your own kung fu movie with legendary characters inspired by martial arts movies across the decades. Whether you fancy playing as an old wise master, a young gifted female kung fu student, a Shaolin monk, a Bruce Lee inspired character, a samurai, or a ninja -- there's bound to be someone for you.

Symmetric Physics Based Combat

"When you learn how to adapt, you will be unstoppable... When you redirect your opponent's energy: you force him to change. So in essence he defeats himself."

How you fight is up to you! But your opponents might use different strategies also, so you must learn how to foresee their attacks and how best to attack them.

Your opponents all have the same controls as you, no advantage, no disadvantage. Except on easier levels they will have less strength - they will also have their speed and intelligence reduced.

Variety

Fight in different scenes in atmospheres inspired by movies, like the dark Red Flower Cult, the misty bamboo forest, kung fu school, (early 20th century) boxing ring + many more.

Choose from over 40 different characters that have various colorful costumes and their own special moves to learn and use. Every character has their own fighting style to master as well as their own perks and weaknesses.

Full Body Tracking (FBT) is fully integrated into Dragon Fist: VR Kung Fu allowing you to fully control both legs while playing! Perform front kicks, knee strikes, or even show off with some flying high kicks! Fully match your opponents with FBT and have the Kung Fu battle you've always dreamed of.

LIV is fully integrated into Dragon Fist: VR Kung Fu so you can bring yourself into the game! Use yourself on a greenscreen or your LIV avatar to create amazing content natively in Dragon Fist!

bHaptics is fully integrated into Dragon Fist: VR Kung Fu letting you feel every kick, punch, and special ability! Put on your favorite bHaptics gear and experience the true power of your opponents!

Co-op & PVP Multiplayer Gamemodes

Dragon Fist: VR Kung Fu takes multiplayer combat to new heights, blending the intensity of classic kung fu cinema with physics based VR combat. Grab your friends and take on both cooperative and competitive experiences that are as intense as they are immersive. Almost everything in single player is available in multiplayer such as: all character, levels, abilities, and more! Play together to beat the campaign or play competitively in custom matches!

Multiplayer modes are about more than just fighting; it's about you and your friends. Step into an 8+ player interactive multiplayer lobby that serves as a social hub, allowing you to connect with friends and foes alike. Host public or private matches to share your experiences, challenges, and triumphs with a global community of fighters!

So, take a deep breath, make a fist, and prepare to battle your way to victory

Fighter Forge Available Now!

Create your own characters in Dragon Fist: VR Kung Fu! This mode allows you to design and motion capture your own characters to use across single and multiplayer modes, as well as share them with the community.

Here’s how it works:

  • Customize your fighter’s appearance, including face, hair, clothing, and poses.

  • Select attributes such as special moves, weaknesses, and perks.

  • Motion capture your own attacking moves.

  • Record your own voice lines from your headset to give your fighter personality.

  • Show off your style and moves in matches!
