Description

Welcome to RUMBLE!

RUMBLE is a VR multiplayer fighting game where you get earthbending powers! Earn Battle Points by defeating other players in intense 1-on-1 matches in the arena, rank up, and unlock more powers to play with!

Earthbending in VR

Your new earthbending abilities are performed by making movements inspired by real martial arts. All abilities have their own pose you need to hit just right in order to use them effectively. When you’ve mastered the movements, you can start combining them together to attack your opponent, and defend yourself from their strikes!

Even when you’ve unlocked all of the moves available to you, there will still be much to learn. Keep fighting, and you will continue to get better and better!

RUMBLE also has…

  • The Park

Besides 1v1 arena matches, you can also go to the Park! Up to 6 players can join a Park, where you can then train together, play mini-games, host tournaments, or play with the toys scattered all throughout the area!

  • Cross-Platform Play

RUMBLE features cross-platform play, meaning that if you play on PC but a friend plays on Standalone VR hardware, you will still be able to play together!

  • Character Customization

RUMBLE features a big character customization system for those who really enjoy making their own characters look interesting and unique. At the Gear Market, you can trade in Gear Coins (which you earn by playing online) for new items to customize your character with!

  • The Rock Cam

The Rock Cam is an in-game camera you can use to take pictures and record gameplay clips! Take pictures with your friends or share your best matches, make memories!

Start your RUMBLE earthbending journey today!
