Description

Build. Automate. Transcend Reality.

Idle Dyson Swarm is a deep incremental game where you construct an ever-expanding network of facilities, harness the power of mega-structures, and push the boundaries of existence itself. Start with simple assembly lines and evolve into a civilization capable of building Galactic Brains that span the cosmos.

A Universe of Automation

Begin your journey producing bots and generating science. Build Assembly Lines, deploy AI Managers, establish Data Centers, and colonize Planets. Each facility feeds into the next, creating satisfying production chains that scale exponentially.

But the real game begins when you unlock Mega-Structures:

  • Matrioshka Brains — Stellar-scale computing structures that consume entire planets

  • Birch Planets — Supermassive planetary shells housing unimaginable potential

  • Galactic Brains — The ultimate achievement, requiring mastery of multiple mega-structure types simultaneously

Three Layers of Prestige

Progress never truly ends. When you've pushed your facilities to their limits, prestige to earn permanent upgrades across three distinct systems:

Infinity — Reset your run to earn Infinity Points. Unlock Secrets of the Universe and permanent facility multipliers that make each subsequent run faster and more rewarding.

Quantum Leap — Convert Infinity Points into Quantum Points. Unlock automation features, bot multitasking, mega-structure access, and powerful meta-upgrades that fundamentally change how you play.

Reality — The deepest layer. Generate Workers to produce Influence, manipulate the fabric of the universe, and push numbers beyond comprehension.

100+ Skills to Master

A sprawling skill tree offers meaningful choices with real trade-offs:

  • Multiple specialization paths — choose between Purity, Terra, Power, Paragade, Stellar, and more

  • Exclusive skills that lock out alternatives — your build matters

  • Banking mechanics to save skill points across runs

  • Refundable and permanent upgrades to balance short-term and long-term strategies

Dream Simulations

Discover Dream 1 — an alternate reality where you guide civilization from primitive hunters to spacefaring engineers:

  • Progress through the Foundational Era and Information Era

  • Face prestige events: Meteor Storms, AI Risk, Global Warming, and Black Holes

  • Earn Strange Matter to permanently boost your main game progress

  • An entirely separate progression loop within the game itself

Play Your Way

  • True Idle Gameplay — Earn resources offline and return to meaningful progress

  • Deep Active Play — Optimize production chains, plan prestige timing, and theorycraft skill builds

  • Automation Unlocks — Gradually unlock auto-buy features for research, bots, and facilities

  • Exponential Scaling — Watch numbers grow from hundreds to incomprehensible magnitudes

Key Features

  • 6 Facility Types including three universe-spanning mega-structures

  • 11 Research Technologies with repeatable upgrades

  • 100+ Branching Skills across multiple specialization trees

  • 3 Prestige Layers providing endless long-term goals

  • 27 Secrets of the Universe granting permanent multipliers

  • Full Simulation Mode with its own eras, buildings, and prestige mechanics

  • Offline Progress that respects your time

  • Exponential Everything — numbers that never stop growing

From assembly lines to galactic brains. From hunters to space factories. From reality to whatever lies beyond.

How far will you go?
