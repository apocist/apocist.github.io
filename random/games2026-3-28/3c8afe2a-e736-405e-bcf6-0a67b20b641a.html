Description

mtion worlds is your virtual sandbox to create, share, and play fun experiences and games. Enjoy impromptu hangouts, build multiplayer games, create dank interactive memes, and everything in between. Stream virtual concerts, play mini-games, or explore haunted mansions with friends. mtion worlds sets you up for both expansive world building and social play.

Discover Endless Worlds. Dive into an expanding universe of player-made experiences. One moment you may be chilling in a floating treehouse under sunsetting skies, the next you're navigating a bizarre mini-game or exploring the backrooms with a friend. In mtion worlds, there's always something new and weird to do.

Build Anything, Anywhere. We believe that creating is as fun as playing. Use our simple drag-and-drop tools to build your first hangout in minutes, or dive deep into our node graph system to script more complex games and interactions. Whether you're a seasoned indie dev or just have a weird idea you want to bring to life, switching between edit and play is a simple button click away.

Real-Time Social & Live Editing. Invite all your friends (or the world) to experience your game as you build it. Watch in real-time as they react to obstacles and roam around, providing you with instant feedback. Even better, edit your creations on the fly! Your changes update instantly for everyone, making for dynamic Game Master-led adventures and fun collaborative building sessions.

Endless Replayability & Remixing. With a universe of player-created worlds, no two sessions have to be the same. Find a world that inspires you and make it your own by remixing it. The community is constantly building, so fresh experiences are always just a click or remix away. From cooperative puzzle challenges to dedicated role-playing servers, the possibilities are limitless.

Join Our Welcoming Community. mtion worlds is home to creators, gamers, and streamers from all corners of the web. Find your niche with dedicated groups for anime fans, indie game enthusiasts, cosplay communities, music producers, VTubers, and more.

Dive into mtion worlds today. Build, play, remix, and see what we can create together!
