Description

STALCRAFT: X is an exciting online MMOFPS game with Survival Horror elements. It combines open world, RPG and dynamic shooter.

For more than 10 years, our team has been creating a unique living world of Chernobyl Exclusion Zone, that will give you new emotions and impressions.

GAMEPLAY DESCRIPTION

Dive into the world through an atypical for MMO cinematic non-linear storyline, which creates the feeling of passing single-player game in the stalker setting for dozens of hours.

In the Zone, your choices always matter, and you'll be able to try on any role and find an activity to your taste: hunting for artefacts or players with artefacts, exploring territories and caches, trading, science, banditry, faction wars and a huge variety of other PvP and PvE activities.

You'll also have your own hideout for resting and preparing for new raids, which you can equip with various furniture and useful structures for crafting.

LIVING OPEN WORLD

The world of STALCRAFT: X will truly surprise you. In addition to real players creating unique situations for each other, it is filled with NPCs and mutants living their normal lives, as well as factions fighting and trading with each other.

The zone is not only dangerous, but also a very beautiful place — various anomalies affect the view of the area and add a special charm to the post-apocalyptic world.

At all locations you can be shot by players from another faction, but you will not lose your equipment and progress, but will lose most of the resources you've gained during your trip. Thanks to this, you will keep the thrill of each raid, and failures will not be fatal.

UNIQUE COMBINATION OF FPS AND RPG GENRES

The balance of weapons is not typical for open-world MMOs and is rather reminiscent of session shooters like Counter Strike and Call of Duty.

We characterise the shooter component as fast-paced, tied primarily to shooting skills and terrain knowledge.

Over the years, we have managed to combine FPS and RPG elements in a unique combination, which makes STALCRAFT: X special among other games of a similar genre.

PROGRESSION AND OPEN ECONOMY

Don't let the MMO genre scare you. There are no levels in our game, and development happens by obtaining new equipment in many ways, including buying it from other players. STALCRAFT: X has an open economy, where everyone can become a famous merchant without leaving the base! By the way, there are no wipes in our game — this means that your progress will be saved forever and will not be deleted.

DISTRIBUTION MODEL

Our game is distributed for free, but monetisation is organised on a “Pay for time” model.

Any items can be obtained in-game, but the soft monetisation method allows you to speed up some areas of character progression or get cosmetic items.
