Description

Welcome to the bright future of colonization with Cosmos Colonisation Corp™!

You are greeted by the planet Sandrax-66b!

It’s dusty. It’s harsh. It’s ours.

You have arrived on the frontline of exploring a new world.

And although your ship has somewhat… stopped being a ship, that’s no reason to despair. Cosmos Colonisation Corp™ believes in you!

And when we believe — even you will survive.

An open and fully destructible world

Everything you see can be taken apart, destroyed, or set on fire. Experiment. Test. Break engineering ethics.

Exploration of discoveries and anomalies

Unknown object? Mysterious signal? A melted door in the middle of the desert? Of course, it’s safe!

Expand the horizons of corporate knowledge.

Daytime is for work. Nighttime is for survival.

While it’s light — build, fortify, upgrade your gear.

When darkness falls — prepare to face the locals. They’re not inclined to negotiate.

Tactical freedom

Follow orders. Or spectacularly break them.

Sneak, attack head-on, unleash the Rot in the camp, or just burn everything down — Cosmos Colonisation Corp™ supports any choice you make.

Building and fortifications

Platforms, walls, campfires. Build your dream shelter.

Everything can be built. Everything can be lost. Especially if you forget to close the door.

Progress through disk modules

Your experience is valuable! Save it on disks and enhance yourself and your equipment.
