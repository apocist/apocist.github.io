Description

Hack, slash, and smash your way to victory in this 2D sidescrolling arcade adventure from The Behemoth! Play with up to four friends locally or online to defend the kingdom, save the princesses, and crash some castles!

Key Features

  • Unlock more than 25 characters and over 40 different weapons!

  • Level up your character with skill points and adjust Strength, Magic, Defense, and Agility to your liking.

  • Intuitive magic and combo system: unlock an arsenal of new attacks as your character progresses through the game.

  • Local or online co-op gameplay: pick up and continue the adventure with your friends at the drop of a helmet!

  • Collect adorable animal orbs for your companions, each with different abilities to aid you on your journey.

Game Modes

  • Arena: battle other players in team matches or free-for-all!

  • Insane Mode: test your skills in the ULTIMATE campaign challenge!

  • Back Off Barbarian: jump and hop across the level to avoid the enemies in this minigame!
